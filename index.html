Interviews Conducted by Team 14 (Deliverable 1)

This project includes interviews with three individuals, each offering unique perspectives on workplace dynamics. Dagim interviewed MYDAD to explore how the goals and structures of various companies shaped his work experience
Taylor interviewed Vic to understand his challenges with transitioning to production work at Applied Medical and the factors behind his frequent work complaints. Hailee interviewed Boomshakalaka, a blue-collar worker
who maintains a positive attitude despite tough working conditions, to gain insight into his resilience and perspective.

Main Workplace Issues from Interviewees (Deliverable 3)

This report explores workplace issues and effective practices from interviews with MYDAD, Vic, and Boomshakalaka. Key challenges include salary dissatisfaction, work exhaustion, and role misalignment, which impact motivation, retention, and stress levels.
Despite these issues, effective practices like recognition and awards, supportive environments, teamwork, safety prioritization, and strong workplace cultures contribute to job satisfaction and operational efficiency. Recommendations address these challenges while building on existing strengths to enhance overall workplace satisfaction and performance.

Five Practical Recommendations (Deliverable 3)

This report provides practical recommendations for addressing key challenges identified through interviews. It explores strategies like implementing performance management to reduce work exhaustion, adopting transformational leadership to improve management practices, and enhancing organizational culture by integrating adhocracy alongside clan culture.
These tailored recommendations aim to foster a more supportive, innovative, and engaging work environment.
